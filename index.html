The work

Most families have thousands of photographs.
Almost none have a recording of the voice.

Memorycord captures, shapes, and produces the stories that matter most — scored with music, designed with the care of a film, and preserved for the people who deserve to hear them. Not a recording service. A produced cinematic experience. Something you feel.

We conduct intimate interviews, sculpt every moment, and deliver something that makes a room go quiet. The music is chosen for this person, this life, this moment. Every piece is one of a kind.

“Every life contains stories waiting to be heard. We find them.”

We don’t extract stories. We create the conditions for them to surface.

A Memorycord session is unhurried, private, and entirely without judgment. There is no wrong answer. No agenda. No performance required.

We’ve found that when people feel truly free — when the room is open and the pace is theirs — they say things they’ve never said to anyone. Not because we asked the right question. Because for once, nobody needed anything from them.

That’s where the story lives. We simply show up and make space for it.

Our promise

Every recording is stored in encrypted, secure archives. Nothing is ever shared, published, or heard by anyone without your explicit written consent. Not for marketing. Not for anything. It belongs to your family and no one else.

Private by design
Encrypted storage. Restricted access. No third party ever hears your story without written authorization.
Preserved for generations
Secure digital archives for 10 to 25 years — or in perpetuity. Physical backups available. Your voice outlasts everything.
Yours to control
You decide who hears it, when, and how. You can request deletion at any time. The story is yours — always.

We are producers, storytellers, and sound designers who have spent our careers in the service of one thing — the story that deserves to last. We’ve heard personal stories more powerful than any book we’ve read or film we’ve seen. Story is what we all are. It’s what connects us, reveals us, and weaves us together across borders and generations.

Between us we have produced audio drama that has touched millions of lives, won Webby Awards, and brought us alongside some of the greatest storytellers in the world. We know what a story sounds like when it’s told authentically and with passion. We know what’s lost when it isn’t.

We started Memorycord because we kept meeting people whose stories deserved to outlast them. So we built something so that they could.

Every interview is conducted with care. Every narrative is shaped with intention. Together we score, produce, and deliver something that goes far beyond a recording. It’s a cinematic portrait in sound, scored like a film, shaped with care, preserved like the heirloom it is.

We know this might be the only time someone tells this story. We treat it that way. We don’t take what you give us lightly.
